Locations

Photo Credit: Z, R. (2017, December 14). Fortnite Map [Digital image]. Retrieved March 6, 2018, from https://www.epicgames.com/fortnite/forums/filedata/fetch?id=140301&d=1516088983

There are many places to land on Fortnite Battle Royale, and each locations are very different in loot, and the amount of people. Here in this page, you will learn about the different cities and towns in this game.

Snobby Shores

Photo Credit: K. (2018, January 18). Snobby Shores Map [Digital image]. Retrieved March 6, 2018, from https://lootlake.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Snobby-Shores-1.jpg

Being one of the newest locations, Snobby Shores is located on the west side edge of the Fortnite Map. It has five buildings which you can land on, which includes the green house, the pool house, the condo, and normal sub-urban houses. There are many chests in each house, including some hidden ones.

Haunted Hills

Photo Credit: Slide. (2018, January 19). Haunted Hills Map [Digital image]. Retrieved March 6, 2018, from https://i.ytimg.com/vi/HetPUJ1CYc0/maxresdefault.jpg

Located on the top left side of the map. Contains minimal loot and rarely any players land in this location. There is not a lot of loot situated in this area.

Junk Junction

Photo Credit: Burnell, L. (2018, January 26). Junk Junction Map [Digital image]. Retrieved March 6, 2018, from https://assets.vg247.com/current//2018/01/fortnite_JunkJunction1.jpg

Located on top left side of the map. Has the famous "metal lama" on the edge of the island. Has many materials for players to gather.

Pleasant Park

Photo Credit: Bell, L. (2017, November). Pleasant Park Map [Digital image]. Retrieved March 6, 2018, from https://s3.amazonaws.com/indieobscura-www/assets/editorial/2017/11/Fortnite-Battle-Royale-Landing-Zone.jpg

One of the most modern places on the map. Has many households and replicates an average sub-urban community Has many chests in the attic of the houses and around 5-10 players land here per game.

Anarchy Acres

Photo Credit: RebelsCat. (2017, September 26). Anarchy Acres Map [Digital image]. Retrieved March 6, 2018, from https://i.ytimg.com/vi/tzAd3hpFRqA/maxresdefault.jpg

One of the largest areas on the map. Not a lot of buildings here but it is a large field. Contains a decent amount of chests hidden through out barns, shacks and houses.

Tilted Towers

Photo Credit: K. (2018, January 18). Tilted Towers Map [Digital image]. Retrieved March 6, 2018, from https://lootlake.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Tilted-Towers-1.jpg

The most notorious location in the whole game. This is the place where you will definitely find combat within the first few seconds of when you land. It has many tall buildings in which you can land on. It is proven to be very difficult to come out of this city alive, but it is worth it as a lot of loot is concentrated on this location.
