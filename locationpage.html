Locations

There are many places to land on Fortnite Battle Royale, and each locations are very different in loot, and the amount of people. Here in this page, you will learn about the different cities and towns in this game.

Snobby Shores

Being one of the newest locations, Snobby Shores is located on the west side edge of the Fortnite Map. It has five buildings which you can land on, which includes the green house, the pool house, the condo, and normal sub-urban houses. There are many chests in each house, including some hidden ones.

Haunted Hills

Located on the top left side of the map. Contains minimal loot and rarely any players land in this location. There is not a lot of loot situated in this area.

Junk Junction

Located on top left side of the map. Has the famous "metal lama" on the edge of the island. Has many materials for players to gather.

Pleasant Park

One of the most modern places on the map. Has many households and replicates an average sub-urban community Has many chests in the attic of the houses and around 5-10 players land here per game.

Anarchy Acres

One of the largest areas on the map. Not a lot of buildings here but it is a large field. Contains a decent amount of chests hidden through out barns, shacks and houses.

Tilted Towers

The most notorious location in the whole game. This is the place where you will definitely find combat within the first few seconds of when you land. It has many tall buildings in which you can land on. It is proven to be very difficult to come out of this city alive, but it is worth it as a lot of loot is concentrated on this location.

Greasy Grove

This location is similar to a residential neighbourhood. It is located on the wets side of the Fortnite map. It contains a decent amount of loot hidden in houses and other buildings.

Shifty Shafts

The only location which includes an undergound mineshaft. It is situated in the southwest quadrant of the map and contains up to seven chests in the mine.

Flush Factory

This location was designed to look like a large warehouse and factory for toilets. It is located south on the Fortnite map and contains many chests.

Fatal Fields

Similar, to Anarchy Acres Fatal Fields is a big farm with barns and fields. It contains a lot of vehicles for metal, and chests withing the barns and houses. Not a lot of players land in this location.

Moisty Mire

This location is mainly a remote swamp area. It consists of trees which give a large amount of wood when broken. It also includes small wooden cabins with chests. It is situated southeast of the Fortnite Map. There is a wooden crab in this location near the edge of the map.

Retail Row

This location is similar to a sub-urban neighbourhood. There are a lot of houses in which chests are located. Many players land here. There are other buildings such as a gas station, parking lots and a grocery store.

Salty Springs

Great for combat when landing. A decent amount of players land here due to the fact that there are many buildings which contain many chests. It is a central location in Fortnite, and is a a good place to make a fort if the storm closes in on this location.

Dusty Depot

This location contains the least amount of chests due to the fact that it contains three warehouses which at most have one chest. This is where many players encounter other players to have their first combat. It is a central location, and getting on top of the warehouse gives you a great height advantage.

Tomato Town

This location is known for it's large tomato centered around a large building. It contains a decent amount of loot. It is a great starting point for new players wishing to finding not too many players to fight.

Wailing Woods

This location is essentially a large forest. It is good for getting materials to build, because each tree in this location gives 30-60 wooden material. The center of this map includes a labyrinth that makes it hard to navigate through. It is located on the north east side of the map and contains few chests.

Lonely Lodge

This location is located on the far east side of the Fortnite map. It is similar to a campground. It contains the wooden fox statue. It has many wooden buildings and trees for wooden material. It also has large wooden towers to attack players with a height advantage.
